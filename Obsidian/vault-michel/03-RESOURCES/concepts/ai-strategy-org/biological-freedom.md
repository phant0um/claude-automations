# Biological Freedom

A concept from [[03-RESOURCES/entities/dario-amodei]]'s *Machines of Loving Grace* essay. Describes a future state in which AI-accelerated biology gives individuals comprehensive control over their own biological processes — analogous to how prior decades expanded reproductive freedom, weight management, and birth control.

## Scope

- Weight and physical appearance
- Reproductive choices (fertility, conception, embryo selection)
- Lifespan extension (targeting ~150 years)
- Mood, cognition, and emotional baseline
- Prevention of genetic disease in offspring
- Reversal or prevention of age-related decline

## Relationship to Equality

Amodei acknowledges biological freedom raises equity concerns — technologies "only for the rich." He is cautiously optimistic that markets + democratic pressure will broaden access in developed countries, but flags global inequality as a harder problem addressed in his economic development section.

## Connection to Neuroscience

The neuroscience equivalent is "cognitive and mental freedom" — the ability to access higher-quality mental states, tune cognitive function, and potentially experience more moments of revelation, creativity, or transcendence.
