# Powerful AI (Definition)

Dario Amodei's preferred term for what others call AGI. He rejects "AGI" as imprecise and loaded with sci-fi baggage; "Expert-Level Science and Engineering" is his more precise alternative label.

## Defining Properties

An AI model (likely LLM-based, possibly multi-model) that:

1. **Intelligence**: smarter than a Nobel Prize winner across most relevant fields (math, biology, programming, engineering, writing)
2. **Interfaces**: full virtual human interface — text, audio, video, mouse/keyboard, internet access; can order materials, direct experiments, watch/make videos
3. **Autonomy**: executes tasks over hours/days/weeks without hand-holding; acts like a capable employee
4. **Physical reach**: no embodiment but can control robots, lab equipment, or design new tools for itself
5. **Scale**: millions of simultaneous instances at 10–100x human speed; instances can collaborate or work independently

## Summary Phrase
