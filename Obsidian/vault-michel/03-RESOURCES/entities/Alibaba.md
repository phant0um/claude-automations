# Alibaba

Chinese technology conglomerate; Alibaba Group Holding Limited, headquartered in Hangzhou, China.

Relevant to this vault primarily as the producer of the [[03-RESOURCES/entities/qwen|Qwen]] model family and [[03-RESOURCES/entities/AgenticQwen]].

## AI Research Contributions

- **Qwen model family** — large-scale LLM series including Qwen3-8B, Qwen3-30B-A3B (MoE), Qwen3-235B-A22B; covers dense and MoE architectures
- **AgenticQwen** — small agentic model family for industrial tool use; trained with [[03-RESOURCES/concepts/ai-strategy-org/dual-data-flywheel]]
- **SynthAgent framework** — synthetic agentic training data generation (referenced in AgenticQwen paper)
