## Andriy Burkov

Machine learning researcher and author. PhD in agent/AI systems (approx. 20 years ago as of 2026). Known for:

- **"The Hundred-Page Machine Learning Book"** — widely referenced ML primer
- Critical AI commentary on X/Twitter (@burkov), particularly on LLM agent limitations from a decision-theoretic perspective

## Key Positions

- LLM-based agents are not rational agents in the formal sense — they optimize next-token prediction, not expected utility maximization. See [[03-RESOURCES/concepts/llm-ml-foundations/llm-rational-agency-gap]].
- For narrow tasks, LLM imitation of rationality may suffice; for general-purpose problem solving, the architectural gap is fatal and not fixable by prompt engineering.
