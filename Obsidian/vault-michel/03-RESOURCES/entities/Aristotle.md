# Aristotle

Ancient Greek philosopher (384–322 BC), student of Plato, founder of formal logic and the concept of first principles in epistemology.

## Relevance

Defined the **first principle** as "the first basis from which a thing is known" — the irreducible foundational truth from which all further reasoning must be derived. This concept is the origin of modern [[03-RESOURCES/concepts/learning-cognition/first-principles-reasoning]].
