# Charlie Munger

American investor and vice-chairman of Berkshire Hathaway (1924–2023). Known for his "lattice of mental models" framework — the idea that superior thinking comes from holding many fundamental models across disciplines.

## Key Idea

"The human brain works in models. The trick is to have your brain work better than the other person's brain because it understands the most fundamental models."

Most people's [[03-RESOURCES/concepts/learning-cognition/mental-models]] aren't fundamental — they're inherited, copies of copies, received wisdom nobody examined. The competitive edge comes from building from first principles.
