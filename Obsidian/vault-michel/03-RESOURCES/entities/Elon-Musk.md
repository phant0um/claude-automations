# Elon Musk

Entrepreneur; founder/CEO of SpaceX and Tesla, among others. Known for applying [[03-RESOURCES/concepts/learning-cognition/first-principles-reasoning]] to domains traditionally governed by analogy and convention.

## First Principles — SpaceX Example

Applied physics-based first principles to rocket pricing. Instead of accepting the market price as fixed, asked: what are rockets made of? Raw material costs = ~2% of market price. That 98% gap revealed an engineering and manufacturing problem, not a physics constraint — enabling SpaceX to target radical cost reduction rather than incremental improvement.

Quote: "Physics teaches you to reason from first principles rather than by analogy."
