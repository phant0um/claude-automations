# Jack Dorsey

Co-founder of Twitter (now X) and Square (now Block). CEO of Block. Known in the AI context for his thesis on restructuring companies around AI intelligence rather than treating AI as a bolt-on productivity tool.

## Known for

- Co-founding Twitter and Square
- Articulating the "company-as-intelligence" model: every company can now be a Mini-AGI
- Driving Block's organizational redesign from 5 layers to 2–3, collapsing to three roles: IC, DRI, Player Coach
- The emergent roadmap concept: gaps in real-time AI composition become the roadmap automatically

## Key Ideas

- **Hierarchy as information routing:** Management hierarchies exist only to move information between humans. When AI can do this, the justification for hierarchy collapses.
- **Structural vs productivity AI:** Most companies bolt AI onto existing structure. Dorsey argues for rebuilding with intelligence at the core.
- **Prototype-first meetings:** Slide decks replaced by working prototypes modifiable live in the room.
- **CEO as architect:** The CEO's new job is to architect the "company-as-intelligence" and maintain its constant alignment.
