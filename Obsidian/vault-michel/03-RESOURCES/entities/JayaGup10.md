# JayaGup10 (Jaya Gupta)

**Handle:** @JayaGup10 on X (Twitter)

Author of the 2026-05-08 essay "The next biggest moat in AI," arguing that organizational shape — how companies concentrate and compound exceptional people — is the durable competitive moat in an era where AI products and technical advantages are rapidly imitable.

## Key Ideas

- Great companies are organizational inventions that make a **new kind of person possible**
- Companies compete on identity, not just compensation or category
- Story altitude must align with organizational shape — grand narrative inside small shape reads as hot air
- Distinction between being **chosen** (emotional) vs **being seen** (structural: scope, authority, economics)
