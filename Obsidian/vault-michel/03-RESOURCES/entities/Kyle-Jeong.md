# Kyle Jeong

**Kyle Jeong** (@kylejeong on X) is a builder at [[03-RESOURCES/entities/Browserbase]]. Author of the "Autobrowse: The Mythos Moment for Browser Agents is Here" article (2026-04-22), which articulates the browser-agent amnesia problem and introduces [[03-RESOURCES/entities/Autobrowse]] as its solution.

## Key Contributions

- Articulated the **browser-agent amnesia problem**: agents re-discover every site from scratch every run, paying the full discovery tax forever.
- Co-wrote the Autobrowse article with @_shubhankar (Autobrowse creator).
- Previously wrote on [[https://x.com/kylejeong/status/2044878529666662616|how Browserbase builds agents]] — every internal workflow runs through one agent loading markdown skills on demand.
