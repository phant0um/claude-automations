# Nicolas Cole

**Handle:** [@Nicolascole77](https://x.com/Nicolascole77)
**Domain:** Digital product creation, ghostwriting, AI-assisted writing workflows

## Profile

Nicolas Cole is a writer and digital product creator who built a methodology equating high-quality AI prompts with education products written for humans. He teaches writers to leverage their existing clarity-of-thought skills as an AI advantage.

His central argument: if you can teach something clearly to a human, you already have the skill to prompt AI effectively — because the two activities are structurally identical.
