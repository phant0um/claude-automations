# OpenAI

AI research lab and company. Founded 2015, originally as a non-profit; transitioned to capped-profit structure. Creators of GPT series, DALL-E, Codex, and ChatGPT.

## Organizational Significance

Cited by @JayaGup10 as a canonical example of a company that was an **organizational invention**: frontier model training as the gravitational center, with safety, policy, product, infrastructure, and deployment all orbiting that core. The structure made a new kind of researcher possible — someone operating simultaneously at science, product, geopolitics, and civilizational risk.

## Key People

- Sam Altman — CEO
- Ilya Sutskever — co-founder (departed 2024)
