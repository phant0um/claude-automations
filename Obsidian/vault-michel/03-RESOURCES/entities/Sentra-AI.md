# Sentra AI

Website: https://www.sentra.app/

Building **enterprise general intelligence**: a shared intelligence/memory layer that sits on all communication channels, knowledge bases, and agent traces to understand how everyone in an organization actually works — constructing a living world model of the entire company in near real time.

## What They're Building

The core problem: making company knowledge not just searchable but **usable in the moment where work is happening**. The hard part is not connecting to tools — the hard part is preserving the shape of the company's knowledge as it moves across people, teams, artifacts, and systems.

Their architecture is based on the [[03-RESOURCES/concepts/pkm-obsidian/company-brain]] framework:

1. [[03-RESOURCES/concepts/llm-ml-foundations/factual-memory]] — what exists, what happened, who owns it, where is the source
2. Interaction memory — why decisions were made
3. Action memory — what actions were taken

Storage model: [[03-RESOURCES/concepts/pkm-obsidian/semantic-file-system]] — relationships around artifacts as first-class data.
