# Steph Zhang

Partner at [[03-RESOURCES/entities/a16z]] (Andreessen Horowitz), focused on enterprise GTM software and AI-native applications.

Twitter: @steph_zhang

## Key Work

- Co-authored "From System of Record to System of Intelligence" (April 2026) with Gio Ahern and Alex Imm — the a16z thesis that the AI orchestration layer will displace CRM as the primary locus of enterprise GTM value

## Thesis

The same dynamic that turned the Facebook friend graph into API-layer infrastructure for the news feed is now happening to the CRM. The [[03-RESOURCES/concepts/ai-strategy-org/system-of-intelligence]] — not the system of record — is where the next decade of GTM enterprise value will accumulate.
