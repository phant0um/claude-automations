# Dario Amodei

**Type:** Person — CEO, [[03-RESOURCES/entities/anthropic|Anthropic]]

Co-founder and CEO of Anthropic. PhD-level background in biology and neuroscience (published research in mass spectrometry / proteomics, computational neuroscience). Former VP of Research at OpenAI before co-founding Anthropic in 2021.

## Key Positions

- Strongly believes AI risk is real and underestimated — AND that AI upside is equally underestimated
- Prefers term "powerful AI" over "AGI" (rejects sci-fi baggage of the latter)
- Advocates for an "entente strategy" among democracies to ensure liberal values dominate the post-powerful-AI world
- Frames AI progress through the lens of [[03-RESOURCES/concepts/ai-strategy-org/marginal-returns-to-intelligence]] rather than singularity-style discontinuities

## Major Works

- **Machines of Loving Grace** (2024 essay) — Vision of AI transforming biology, neuroscience, economic development, governance, and human meaning. See [[03-RESOURCES/sources/ml-research-papers/dario-amodei-machines-of-loving-grace]].
