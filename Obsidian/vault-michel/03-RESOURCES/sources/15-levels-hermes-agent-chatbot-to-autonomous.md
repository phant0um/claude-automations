## Tese central
Mapa de 15 níveis de uso do Hermes Agent (v0.17.0), agrupados em 3 phases: Foundation (1-3), Automation (4-9), Autonomous System (10-15). Cada level: o que é, o que unlocka, setup, e o mistake que tripa people up. Most people usam 10% do capability.

## Key insights
- 3 phases: Foundation (one-shot prompts), Automation (cron, skills, plugins), Autonomous System (self-evolving, multi-agent)
- Level 1 = chatbot, Level 15 = runs your business without you
- Setup modes: Quick (Nous Portal), Full, Blank Slate (everything OFF, enable only what needed)
