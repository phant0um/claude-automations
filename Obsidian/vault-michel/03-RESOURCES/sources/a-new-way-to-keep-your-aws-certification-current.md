## Argumentos principais
### How it works
When your certification is within 90 days of expiration, you’re eligible. Here’s what the experience looks like:
1. **Choose your certification** — In AWS Skill Builder, navigate to **Explore** > **Validate your skills** > **Recertify** and select the certification you want to maintain
2. **Complete your training** — Work through curated digital courses and hands-on labs covering relevant, evolving topics tied to your certification domain

### Training that keeps your skills current, not just recertified
The courses and labs are built by the same AWS experts who develop AWS services — covering topics that reflect how the cloud is evolving right now, not a rehash of what you studied years ago. The practical activities place you in realistic scenarios where you build, configure, and troubleshoot in live AWS environments. Time spent maintaining your certification is time spent getting better at your job.

### What’s supported at launch
This experience is available today in open Beta for the following certifications:
- AWS Certified Solutions Architect – Associate
- AWS Certified Developer – Associate

### How this fits with exam-based recertification
You can still recertify by retaking your certification exam, passing a higher-level exam, or (for Cloud Practitioner) using AWS Cloud Quest. The ability to maintain your certification through training is an additional path — one that emphasizes continuous, applied learning. Both paths result in an active, valid credential.

### Cascading certification extensions
Maintaining an AWS Certification will automatically extend the expiration date of related, lower-level certifications that are still active to match the expiration date of the certification you maintained. For example, if you maintain your Solutions Architect – Professional, your Solutions Architect – Associate extends with it to match the next expiration date (as long as it’s still active and expiring within 1 year).
What you need to get started
- An active AWS Skill Builder subscription (Individual or Team)

### Get started
Log in to AWS Skill Builder and navigate to [Recertify]() to check your eligibility and start your maintenance path.
This is an offering from the AWS Certification program, delivered through the AWS Skill Builder experience. It represents the next step in how AWS Certification evolves with you — keeping your credential active through the continuous, hands-on learning that cloud professionals have been asking for.
Stay current. Stay certified. Start maintaining your certification today.
