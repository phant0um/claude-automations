# How to Make $11,000/Month with Computer Vision Using AI

**Tese central**: Computer vision comercial (counting, tracking, speed estimation) é acessível sem programação prévia usando YOLO + ByteTrack + Supervision + Roboflow + AI assistants. Mercado de $22B (2024) → $111B (2033). Renda via freelancing internacional (Upwork) em dólares.

## Stack (plain language)

- **YOLO**: o "olho" — modelo pronto que detecta objetos com bounding boxes
- **ByteTrack**: a "memória" — links objects entre frames com IDs persistentes
- **Supervision**: o "kit de construção" — blocks prontos: boxes, counting lines, zones
- **Roboflow**: plataforma quase no-code + universe de modelos pré-treinados
- **Google Colab**: computador na nuvem gratuito com GPU
- **AI assistant (Claude/ChatGPT/Gemini)**: "seu programador"

## Pipeline

Video → YOLO (detect) → ByteTrack (track/ID) → Supervision (count by line/zone) → resultado

## 4 tarefas comerciais core

1. Counting objects (warehouses, inventory)
2. Tracking people/vehicles (stores, roads)
3. Speed estimation (traffic, safety)
4. Sports analytics (player tracking)

## Business model

- **Path B (mais rápido)**: Freelancing/turnkey projects no Upwork
- **Path A (mais lento)**: Full-time engineering job
- Rates: Junior $50-80/hr, Middle $80-120, Senior $120-200+
- Turnkey: $300-1500 (simples) a $5k+ (sérios), mercado chega a $250k+
- 90 days plan: Week 1 (Colab), Weeks 2-3 (demos), Week 4 (GitHub), Week 5 (Upwork), Weeks 6-10 (proposals), Weeks 11-13 (deliver + reviews)
