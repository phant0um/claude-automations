## Tese central
Claude é weak com one sentence + no context. 7 setup layers: (1) Project (workspace, knowledge base), (2) docs/examples, (3) current sources, (4) connected tools, (5) subagents, (6) approval rules, (7) boundaries. Material to reason from + tools to act with + boundaries to stay inside.

## Key insights
- Project setup: 1 source-of-truth doc, 1 audience doc, 1 style doc, 1 decision log, 1 instruction block
- "Claude carries the job across chats" com Projects
- Order importa: setup layers em sequência
