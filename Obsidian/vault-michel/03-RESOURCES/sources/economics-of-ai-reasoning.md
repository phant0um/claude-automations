## Tese central
Reasoning models (o1, DeepSeek-R1, o3) foram force multiplier early on. Em 2025 quase todo modelo tem reasoning support. Economics de reasoning: tradeoff entre accuracy e cost (tokens de thinking).

## Key insights
- Reasoning = "thinking", "curfuffling", "cooking" — force multiplier quando LLMs discovering tool use
- O1/O3 crunching problems for hours before returning mostly accurate responses
- Quase todo modelo em 2025 tem reasoning support
