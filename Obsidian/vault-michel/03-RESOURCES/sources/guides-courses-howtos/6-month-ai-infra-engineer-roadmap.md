## Tese central
Roadmap 12 estágios de Linux/Networking até deploy de sistemas reais de IA para virar AI Infra Engineer em 6 meses.

## Key insights
- Stages 1-4: Linux+Redes, Python async/FastAPI, GPU básicos (CUDA/VRAM/quant), LLM inference (vLLM, TensorRT-LLM, speculative decoding, KV cache).
- Stages 5-8: distributed systems, AI serving (streaming, rate limit, observability), data pipelines (Kafka/Airflow/vector index), K8s+Cloud.
- Stages 9-12: monitoring (Prometheus/Grafana/cost tracking), sistemas reais (RAG, inference clusters), OSS contrib, candidatar.
