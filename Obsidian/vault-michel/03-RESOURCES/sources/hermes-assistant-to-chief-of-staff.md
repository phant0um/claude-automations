## Tese central
Hermes como worker que você roda, não chat window que você abre. 12 jobs: branded artifacts on demand (design.md), run inside Slack, books sales calls, revives stalled deals, ships ad creative. Agente gerencia outros agentes underneath = assistant → operator.

## Key insights
- "Think of Hermes as a worker you run rather than a chat window you open"
- design.md do Google: point agent at it, every visual sai on brand
- Branded artifact stick better que wall of Slack text
- Slack > side app para team work: threaded replies organizam messy back-and-forth
